About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher and PhD student at the University of Turku, conducting research within the Trienkatu lab. My academic work focuses deeply on autonomous systems and robotics, specifically specializing in the joint optimization of mechanical and computational energy efficiency in autonomous electric vehicle swarms. To train and evaluate multi-agent reinforcement learning models, I leverage advanced simulation frameworks such as NVIDIA Isaac Sim and Isaac Lab.
