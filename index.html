About Me

I am a doctoral researcher in the Autonomous Systems Laboratory at the University of Turku, working on energy-aware optimization for autonomous electric vehicles (AEVs) and mobile robot fleets. My research jointly models mechanical propulsion energy, onboard computational energy (perception, localization, and planning), and battery degradation, and uses evolutionary algorithms combined with customized path-search methods to optimize routing, motion parameters, and compute settings for individual robots and multi-vehicle swarms.
